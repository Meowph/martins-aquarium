Tank Maintenance and Cleaning Tips

  • Check salinity Use a refractometer or hydrometer to regularly test your tank's salinity and specific gravity levels. This will help you identify any shifts that need to be corrected

  • Add heated freshwater If your tank has heated water, you can add heated freshwater to correct for water loss through evaporation

  • water For the first few months, you should replace 10-25% of the water weekly. You can use a gravel or sand siphon to access the deeper layers of the substrate

  • Clean the tank Vacuum the bottom of the tank to remove sediment, and scrub algae away. You can also clean the cover glasses, lighting, and acrylic shield

  • Test other water quality Check the pH, alkalinity, ammonia, nitrite, and nitrate levels of the water

  • Inspect filter media and pumps Make sure each filter and pump is operating correctly
